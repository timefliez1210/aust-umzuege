# SEO Hooks — All Service Pages

## The Seniorenumzug Formula (What We're Replicating)

Seniorenumzug dominates because it has a **concrete, specific, verifiable claim** in its meta that no competitor makes:

> "Bis zu **4.180 € Zuschuss** von der Pflegekasse möglich"

This works because it:
1. Names a **specific € figure** (not "günstig" or "fair")
2. References a **specific legal basis** (§40 SGB XI — Pflegekasse)
3. Creates **entirely new long-tail queries** nobody else ranks for
4. Answers the searcher's #1 question in the SERP snippet

Every hook below follows this same pattern: **verifiable claim + specific figure + legal/institutional reference**.

## 1. Privatumzug — THE BROKEN ONE (Fix First)

### Current (broken)
- **Title:** `Umziehen in Hildesheim – Festpreis, Stressfrei & Zuverlässig`
- **Problem:** "Privatumzug" doesn't even appear. Google can't match this to the query "Privatumzug Hildesheim".
- **Description:** Generic social proof. "Nahezu alle unserer Kunden empfehlen uns..." — every competitor says this.

### Proposed
- **Title:** `Privatumzug Hildesheim – Steuerlich absetzbar | Aust Umzüge`
- **Description:** `Privatumzug Hildesheim mit Festpreisgarantie. Bei beruflichem Umzug bis zu 1.034 € Pauschale steuerlich absetzbar (§9 EStG). 2-Zimmer ab ~600 €. Kostenlos besichtigen lassen.`

### Fact verification
| Claim | Source | Verifiable? |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 1.034 € Umzugskostenpauschale | §9 Abs. 2 Nr. 5 EStG + BMF-Schreiben vom 14.12.2023 (BStBl. 2024 I S. 57) — aktualisierte Pauschale für beruflich bedingte Umzüge | ✅ BMF publishes this |
| §9 EStG — Werbungskosten | §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 6 EStG: Umzugskosten bei beruflicher Veranlassung als Werbungskosten absetzbar | ✅ Gesetz |
| 2-Zimmer ab ~600 € | Already on the page: FAQ says "2-Zimmer-Umzug erfahrungsgemäß zwischen 400 und 800 Euro" | ✅ Own data |
| Festpreisgarantie | Already on the page, core business promise | ✅ Own data |

### Why this works
- **"Steuerlich absetzbar"** is the Pflegekasse-equivalent hook: a hidden financial benefit from bureaucracy that most people don't know about
- **§9 EStG** creates new long-tail territory: "umzug steuerlich absetzbar", "umzugskosten pauschale", "umzug beruflich absetzen"
- **Specific € figure** (1.034 €) creates the same CTR-boosting effect as "4.180 €"
- **Note:** The current page FAQ says "964 Euro" — this is outdated. The BMF updated the Pauschale to 1.034 € as of 2023. The FAQ should also be updated.

### Bonus: §35a EStG angle for private moves
For privately motivated moves (not beruflich), §35a Abs. 2 EStG allows a tax credit of **20% of labor costs, up to 4.000 € per year** for haushaltsnahe Dienstleistungen. This is the second tax hook. Could be used as:
> `Auch privat: 20 % der Arbeitskosten steuerlich zurück (§35a EStG)`

This is the **exact analog** to the Pflegekasse hook — a government subsidy most people don't know about. Consider adding this to the body text and a FAQ question.

## Secondary: §35a EStG Content Addition (Privatumzug)

The Seniorenumzug page's success comes from developing its hook across meta + FAQ + body. The Privatumzug page mentions §35a EStG (haushaltsnahe Dienstleistungen) only once, buried in a single FAQ answer. To replicate the Pflegekasse formula, add:

1. **New FAQ question:** "Kann ich Umzugskosten als haushaltsnahe Dienstleistung von der Steuer absetzen?"
   - Answer: Yes — §35a Abs. 2 EStG allows 20% of labor costs as tax credit, up to 4.000 € per year. Applies to privately motivated moves. Requires invoice with separately listed labor costs. We provide this automatically.

2. **Body mention:** Add one paragraph under the "Privatumzug in Hildesheim" section about the tax benefits, naming both §9 EStG (beruflich) and §35a EStG (privat).

This creates the same topical cluster that makes Seniorenumzug the authority for Pflegekasse queries — but for Umzug + Steuern queries.
